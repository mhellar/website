Created for the interactive agency, ChapterSF, the Converse Beacon was a Bluetooth enabled wearable in a custom 3d printed enclosure that would provide visual notifications about the weather and other incoming data services.

"Converse asked us to hack a pair of Chucks around the theme of weather. We created Beacon - an Arduino powered wearable that puts the weather forecast on your feet. Unlike other Chuck Hacks that are just for show, we built an authentic device that anyone with a pair of Chucks, a soldering iron and a lazy afternoon, could hack together."
