scenia

  • Shadow Stalker, 2019

    Installation

  • Client
    Lynn Hershman Leeson, Artist

  • Role
    Software Development and Installation Design

In Shadow Stalker, Hershman Leeson’s new commission for The Shed in New York City, the artist tackles predictive algorithms, weaponized by corporations and governments to predict, score, and rank one’s future employability and loan­ worthiness. Through the artist’s collaboration with programmers Mark Hellar and Rachel Rose Ulgado, visitors are first invited to enter their email address into an iPad to get an acute sense of their data shadow, or the data that is collected and stored in the course of daily life.
