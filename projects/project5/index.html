  • Lovers, Teiji Furuhashi 1994

    conservation

  • Client
    MoMA

  • Role
    Electronic Engineering and Software Development

Lovers is an immersive, room-sized installation that projects carefully choreographed videos of the artist and collaborators traveling along the walls of a darkened room. At the center of the installation is a tall metal computer-controlled tower with stepper motor-driven turntables that facilitates the movent of the projectors. In preparation for the 2016 re-exhibition of the work, MoMA contracted me to re-engineer the electronics for the motor control system using a modern microcontroller based system and develop custom software-based video players to replace the original laserdisc playback system.
